Our Story

Born in Kampala,
Worn Across Africa

Kampala Threads was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate Ugandan fashion designers who believed that African fashion deserved a modern, digital home.

What started as a small boutique in Nakasero Market has grown into Uganda's leading online fashion platform — connecting local tailors with customers across the country and beyond.

Our mission is simple: celebrate African craftsmanship, support local artisans, and make authentic Ugandan fashion accessible to everyone.

🧵
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🏆 Quality First

Every garment undergoes strict quality checks before it reaches you. We partner only with skilled artisans who take pride in their craft.

🤝 Community Driven

We are committed to uplifting Ugandan artisans, designers, and fabric traders. Every purchase supports a local family and their livelihood.

🌿 Sustainable Fashion

We champion eco-friendly production methods, using natural dyes and fabrics that are kind to Uganda's beautiful natural environment.
